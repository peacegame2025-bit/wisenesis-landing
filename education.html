Advanced Formula Design

Advanced Formula Design is the flagship Wisenesis course, created for experienced TCM students and practitioners who want to deepen their prescription logic and real-world herbal decision-making.

Instead of memorizing formulas, you will learn how to:

  • Identify the core pathomechanism in complex cases
  • Select a stable base formula
  • Layer add-ons, modifications, and sub-formulas
  • Adjust dosage and structure over multi-visit follow-ups
  • Build a replicable clinical thinking model

Who this course is for

  • Senior TCM students preparing for clinic
  • Herbalists wanting deeper formula skills
  • Acupuncturists who want stronger herbal reasoning
  • Doctors treating chronic internal medicine patterns
