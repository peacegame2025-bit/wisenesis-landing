Advanced Formula Design Masterclass

Advanced Formula Design is the flagship Wisenesis course, created for TCM students and practitioners who want to deepen their clinical prescription logic, classical foundations, and real-world herbal decision-making.

Instead of memorizing formulas, this course teaches you how to:

  • Identify the core pathomechanism in complex cases
  • Select a stable base formula as the “spine”
  • Layer add-ons, modifications, and secondary formulas
  • Adjust dosage and structure during follow-ups
  • Build a clinical thinking model that is repeatable

Who This Course Is For

  • Senior TCM students preparing for clinic
  • Acupuncturists wanting deeper herbal skills
  • Herbalists seeking structured formula training
  • Doctors who treat chronic internal medicine patterns
