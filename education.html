Education Philosophy

Wisenesis education is built to train clinical thinking — not just recall. The goal is to help learners move from “Which formula should I use?” to “What structural direction does this case require?”

How We Teach

  • Classical foundations + modern medical reasoning
  • Pathomechanism-first case reading (not symptom checklists)
  • Stable structure: base formula as a “spine”, then layered strategy
  • Follow-up logic: adjustment principles and decision checkpoints
  • Repeatability: a framework you can apply across cases
